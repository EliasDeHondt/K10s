About the K10s Founders

The K10s project was born out of a passion for open-source software and the desire to simplify the management of Kubernetes clusters. The founders of K10s, a group of developers and Kubernetes enthusiasts, sought to create a free and intuitive solution to help developers and sysadmins alike manage and monitor their Kubernetes environments.

The core values of K10s revolve around community-driven development, transparency, and accessibility. As an open-source project, K10s encourages contributions from developers around the world. The founders firmly believe that by sharing knowledge and resources, the open-source community can grow and innovate faster, ultimately benefiting everyone in the tech world.
